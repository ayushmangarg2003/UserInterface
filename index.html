About us

We are DocOnTap

DocOnTap is an Appointment App. It allows you to book appointments to see Doctors of different speciality by listing them in order of closest Distance. You can take a time slot and confirm your meeting with Doctor. DocOnTap also allows Doctors and Diagnostic centres to list their services. We wish to give a level playing field to all the Healthcare workers by incorporating Remote communication and Blogging for Medical Students and Doctors. You can also check the Live Bed availiability in Hospitals around you.
